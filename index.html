Background

Across Universties, introductory computer science courses face high drop out rates. The University of Cape Town is no stranger to this phenomenon and has introduced an Extended Degree Program course, CS1010H, to provide struggling first year computer science students with the support they require. Retention of students is vital not only to the growth of the field, but to the many disciplines, adacemic and commercial, that require computer science graduates.
